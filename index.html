The Details

About 408 West 34th Street

408 West 34th St, also known as Madison Gardens, is a 6 story cooperative that was built in 1963. It was converted into cooperative in 1989. The building features an elevator, laundry, storage units, live-in super, and an intercom system. There is a surface parking lot behind the coop which currently has a wait-list. The building is pet friendly and has low maintenance. Subletting is permitted, however, there is a monthly fee which is determined based off the number of years a shareholder has owned the unit.

Resident alert program

Resident Alerts & Consent

Madison Gardens offers optional SMS, email, and automated voice alerts for building-related communications (such as maintenance work, service interruptions, safety notices, and other time-sensitive updates).

How to enroll

Residents may opt in by signing the Resident Alerts Consent Form located in the building lobby. The form asks for your:

  • Name
  • Apartment number
  • Mobile phone number
  • Email address

By signing the form, you consent to receive:

  • Text messages (SMS)
  • Automated voice calls
  • Email notifications

from the Madison Gardens Co-op Board and Eisenstein Realty regarding building-related communications.

Message frequency & opt-out

Message frequency: generally up to 2 alerts per month, though frequency may temporarily increase during urgent building situations.

Opt-out: you may opt out at any time by replying STOP to a text message, or by emailing the board at madisongardensco-opboard@gmail.com.

Proof of consent

The Co-op Board and managing agent maintain copies of signed consent forms, which include the date of signing, your contact information, and your signature. These records serve as proof of opt-in for our messaging provider and the wireless carriers.

Your contact details are used only for building-related communications and are not sold or shared for marketing purposes.
